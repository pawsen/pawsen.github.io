I was on Tenerife in the first months of 2020 when covid hit.
Lucky for me, when most tourist left the island, I stayed, got to know locals and climb with the guides for half a year.

Two years later I was back for a another climbing trip, this time with a friend. What I thought would be 6 weeks on the island turned out to be another half year.
